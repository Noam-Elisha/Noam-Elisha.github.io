Hi, I'm Noam

I'm a violinist and teacher based in Berkeley, California. I perform as a section violinist with the Sacramento Philharmonic & Opera, and I teach private violin lessons to students of all ages and levels. Music has been at the center of my life for as long as I can remember, and I love sharing that with others.

As a teacher, I'm patient, structured, and always adapting to each student's goals and learning style. Whether you're picking up the violin for the very first time or preparing for an audition, I believe music should be enjoyable at every stage. My lessons balance technique with expression, so you're always making progress while having fun.

I received a fellowship to the Aspen Music Festival & School and hold a B.A. in Music from UC Berkeley, where I served as conductor and concertmaster of the UC Berkeley Philharmonia Orchestra, assistant conductor of the UC Berkeley Symphony Orchestra, and was a finalist in the Cal Concerto Competition.
